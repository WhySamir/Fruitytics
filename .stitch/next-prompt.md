## Objective
Generate the **Lab Archives & Analytics Dashboard** for FruitVerse Nutrition. This is where users see their historical data, trend lines for nutrient intake, and system performance benchmarks.

## Visual Vibe
Data-Dense, Clinical, Monochromatic with Safety Accents. High-contrast data visualization.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- **Atmosphere:** Industrial Precision Laboratory.
- **Palette:** Foundry Black (#09090B), Machined Steel (#18181B), Brushed Zinc (#27272A), Cold Concrete (#A1A1AA), Optical White (#FAFAFA), Safety Orange (#F97316).
- **Shape:** 0px border-radius (Strictly sharp).
- **Typography:** Outfit (Display/Body), Geist Mono (Numeric Data/Labels).

## Page Structure
1. **Header:** FRUITVERSE // LAB (Consistent with other pages).
2. **Top Stats Row:** Four sharp blocks showing:
   - TOTAL_SAMPLES (numeric).
   - AVG_CONFIDENCE (98.4%).
   - UPTIME (99.99%).
   - STORAGE_INDEX (0xA42).
3. **Main Data Visual:** A large technical chart (placeholder as a stylized line/bar graph) showing "Nutrient Trends Over Time".
4. **Recent Logs List:** A vertical list of analyzed fruits (e.g., Dragonfruit, Mangosteen, Pomelo) with timestamps and confidence scores.
5. **System Health Sidebar:** Compact blocks showing hardware status and neural model version info.
